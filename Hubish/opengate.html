Time Loops

Time loops are anomalies where a sequence of events repeats endlessly.

  • Repetition of events within a fixed period.
  • Extra memory retention by some individuals within the loop.
  • Potential to alter the sequence with interventions.
  • Can be created through tears in time or extreame phychological tiggers such as stress or guilt

Resolution Protocol:

  1. Using tools such as an M-Eraser to remove affecting memories
  2. Altering details of the event chain.
  3. Using a Time Syncer (Last ditch effort as it may completely destroy user and those effected)

Temporal Immunity

Temporal immunity occurs when individuals become impervious to physical harm or changes in their timeline. Casued due to outside munipulation.

Resolution Protocol:

  1. Ripping specific targets out of the anomalies (will create a huge time scent on them)
  2. Split the person through time a kill each varient individually.
  3. Force into a time loop and never think about them again.

Temporal Distortions

Temporal distortions involve anomalies where individuals or environments behave unpredictably, often losing coherence with the timeline.

Key Indicators:

  • Erratic memory loss or gain.
  • Environmental inconsistencies.
  • Unusual shifts in time perception.

Resolution Protocol:

  1. Lock time line.
  2. Neuro modification and attempt to anchor new memories within subject.
  3. Use a Time Syncer (Last ditch effort as it may completely destroy user and those effected)
