Time Anomalies Training Manual

Introduction to Time Anomalies

Welcome to the Time Anomalies Training Manual. This guide will help you understand the nature of time anomalies, how to identify them, and the procedures for managing them effectively. Time anomalies are unpredictable and often dangerous phenomena that can distort the fabric of time. It is important as a soldier to maintain client sanity and pull them out of their event.

Understanding Time Anomalies

Time anomalies can manifest in various forms, including:

  • Time Loops: A client experiences repeated events or periods of time that seem to loop.
  • Temporal Immunity: A client becomes unable to perish due to anything other than old age, causing major warps in time.
  • Temporal Distortions: Some clients become unable to understand basic knowledge and must undergo treatment.

Response Protocols

It is essential to follow a structured protocol when encountering a time anomaly. Below are the key steps to follow:

  • Step 1: Identify the nature of the anomaly (loop, immunity, other).
  • Step 2: Secure the point in time.
  • Step 3: Report the anomaly to TAC higher-ups immediately.
  • Step 4: Follow instructions from the TAC team for neutralizing or containing the anomaly.
  • Step 5: Don't step out of line.
